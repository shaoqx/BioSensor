PeanutPal

Speed

  • Depends on the binding speed of the antibody
  • All other components are essentially instantaneous
  • Waiting 20 minutes after adding the sample to the device
  • Future experimentation can determine if incubation time can be shortened

Ease and convenience

  • Household/handheld device
  • Comparable to a glucose monitor for diabetes patients

Skill required of operator

  • Young teen or younger should be able to easily use the device
  • Device is handheld and can be used anywhere
  • Simple instructions

Cost for using the device

Device $400

  • Regeneration buffer
  • 1 mL distilled water in sample tube
  • 20 uL PBS in sample tube
  • Plastic syringe

Recharge Kit $100
Recharge after 20 uses for 2 weeks

  • glutaraldehyde solution to wash away existing antibodies
  • an antibody solution to replace them
  • standard solutions for calibration
